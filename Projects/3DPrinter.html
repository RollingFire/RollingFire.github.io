3D Printer

I was introduced to 3D printers my first year of college. I took a class to learn Solid Works so that I can design things to print on it, and for one of my classes, I printed a boat that would float. Over the summer after my first year, I bought a kit to build my own 3D printer. The kit ended up being a cheap kit from China that didn’t come with instructions and gave me a lot of problems, and it took a lot of jerry-rigging to get working. I got it working though. It still needs some more work to get it more accurate and get higher detail, but I haven’t taken the time to figure out what needs to be done to it and do it.
