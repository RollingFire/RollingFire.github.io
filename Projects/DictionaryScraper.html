Dictionary Scraper

To soothe my curiosity of which word is used most often to describe words in the dictionary, I created a web scrapper to scrap dictionary.com to find out for me. It turns out that other than words such as 'a', 'in', 'by', 'is', 'the', 'for', etc., the most used words for descriptions on dictionary.com as of 4/15/18 are 'especially' and 'person'.

  • Dealing with request timeouts

This was a fun project for me. For some reason I was curious what word is most used to describe other words. It wasnâ€™t too hard to get it working. The first time I ran it, however, it was painfully slow. Most page requests took about a second, but some would take a few more seconds. Across the entire list of words, I think I estimated that it would take 10 days total. I found that the bottleneck was in the delay in sending and receiving the site requests and not in the data transfer. My solution was to multithread the program to request multiple pages at once so that the bottleneck would be the bandwidth of my internet. With this new configuration it took approximately 11 hours.
